Summary

"The Dark Knight" is a gripping superhero thriller that follows Batman as he confronts the Joker, a criminal mastermind spreading chaos in Gotham City. As the Joker pushes the city into anarchy, Batman must balance his heroic duty with personal sacrifices, testing the limits of his morality and justice.

Actors

Christian Bale stars as Bruce Wayne / Batman, supported by an outstanding cast including Heath Ledger as the Joker, Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent, Gary Oldman as Commissioner Gordon, and Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth.
