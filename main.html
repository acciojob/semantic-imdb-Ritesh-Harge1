The Shawshank Redemption

(1994 prison drama)

Summary

Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murdering his wife, is sentenced to life at Shawshank Prison. Over the years, he befriends Red, earns the trust of prisoners and guards, and ultimately orchestrates a brilliant escape.

Main Actors

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne – a quietly resilient banker.
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis “Red” Redding – Andy’s loyal friend and the story’s narrator.
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Norton – a devout yet corrupt warden.

Aside: Their relationships drive the emotional core of the film.

Favourite Quote

“Hope is a good thing, maybe the best of things, and no good thing ever dies.” – Andy Dufresne

This highlights the central theme: hope transcends even the bleakest circumstances.

Note: The friendship between Andy and Red is the heart of this film.
