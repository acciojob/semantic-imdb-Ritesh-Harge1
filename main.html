Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller that explores the world of shared dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from within the subconscious during dreams, is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform inception—planting an idea into someone's mind.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, and Cillian Murphy in supporting roles. The cast delivers compelling performances that enhance the complex narrative.
