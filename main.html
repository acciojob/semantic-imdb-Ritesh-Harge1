Summary

"The Shawshank Redemption" tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongly convicted of murder and sent to Shawshank prison. Over the decades, he forms a friendship with fellow inmate Red and maintains hope despite the harsh prison life, ultimately orchestrating a daring escape that brings justice and redemption.
