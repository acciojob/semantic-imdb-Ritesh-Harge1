Summary

"Interstellar" is a science fiction epic where Earth is facing environmental collapse. A team of astronauts, led by Cooper, travels through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. The film explores themes of time dilation, love, and survival across space and time.

Actors

The film stars Matthew McConaughey as Joseph Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Dr. Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murphy Cooper, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand. Each actor delivers a compelling performance that adds emotional depth to the scientific narrative.
